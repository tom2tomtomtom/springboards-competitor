03 / 12

What Springboards actually is

An ideation chat app. Great logo, great content, shallow moat.

The product: "Flint," a proprietary LLM tuned for creative variation. Chat + shared canvas + custom profiles + monthly Spark Sessions (webinars with James Hurman, Zoe Scaman, Tom Roach).


Pricing: Freemium → $30 Pro → $45/seat Team → Enterprise (SSO). Classic seat-based SaaS. A 20-seat agency = US$10.8k/yr.


Revenue (reverse engineered): 120 agencies × avg 5–15 seats × ~US$50/mo = ~US$0.5–1m ARR at seed, ~US$1.5–3m today.


Team: ~27 people (24 staff + 3 founders).

The Tom read (Apr 2026, direct from Pip)

"This year has been really hard." — Pip Bingemann, April 2026.


The pattern is clear from Tom's client base: nearly every agency Tom works with has tried or subscribes to Springboards. Almost none use it a month later.


Good at getting in. Weak at staying in. A shallow wrapper on a frontier LLM. Variation is not a moat when GPT-5 and Claude Opus close the gap every quarter.

09 / 12

What we take from Springboards' playbook

Five things. One thing we don't.

1. Named authority guests

They rent Hurman / Scaman / Roach credibility via Spark Sessions. AIDEN equivalent: closed-room monthly session with 3–4 agency leaders + Russ as convenor.

2. A public POV, not a brochure

Their whole site takes an editorial stance ("sameness is the enemy"). AIDEN's stance: "Tools don't make you AI-native. Rewiring the shop does."

3. Distribution speed

They got into 120 agencies in 24 months without engineers. Russ + Chris + Matt is our version of that engine.

4. Founder-visible brand

Three founders front-and-centre. AIDEN's About = Tom + Russ + Matt + Chris. Buyers pick the humans first.

5. Category-defining language

They own "creative variation." AIDEN owns "the phantom brain" and "the argument the machine executes." Say it every week until it sticks.

What AIDEN doesn't copy

Seat-based SaaS at $45/mo. Freemium. 24 people on payroll. Chasing enterprise via SSO checkboxes. That's their game, margin-thin and cat-and-mouse with OpenAI. AIDEN's game is fewer, deeper, higher-ACV, IP-defended. Different fight.
